Who I Am

I am a Multimedia and Data Analytics Developer who blends creativity with technical problem solving. I enjoy bridging the gap between design, development, and data to create solutions that feel both smart and human centered.

Full-stack applications with Python, Java, JavaScript, and SQL. I also specialize in processing large datasets exceeding 10,000 rows, using Power BI, Talend, and enterprise systems to automate workflows and deliver actionable insights.

I start by breaking problems into smaller pieces and tackling them one at a time. I like writing clean, readable code and testing as I go. Whether it's a solo project or a team sprint, I stay organized with version control and clear documentation so nothing falls through the cracks.

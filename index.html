About Me

I am a Multimedia and Data Analytics Developer who blends creativity with technical problem solving. My work focuses on building digital experiences that are intuitive, visually engaging, and grounded in strong engineering principles. I enjoy bridging the gap between design, development, and data to create solutions that feel both smart and human centered.

My goal is always to turn raw information into clear, actionable narratives.
